Project Info

  • The project provides the ability to analyse and download the complete report based on the question paper (docx) uploaded by the user.


  • The analysis is displayed in form of various charts and graphs to provide the user with the statistics of the paper and decide the quality of the question paper.


  • The automated system to analyse paper enables the faculties to determine the quality of the question paper.


  • The system considers the difficulty level of each question and divides the total marks of a question in a different level based on a defined level weight ratio.


  • The same is done for all the questions of the question paper and the total weight of all the level is calculated in percentage and the various type of chart is displayed.


  • The analysed data is used to generate an overall report and enables the user to download it.


  • The report and the paper both are saved in the database for historical references and to enable the user to download and view past paper and its analysis.
