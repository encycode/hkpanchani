Project Info

Our app contains different types of body workouts that you can watch and learn easily and It will allow you to achieve great results in no time. It has most of the gym workouts like Chest, Biceps, Shoulders, etc. Gifs which we have put here can show you which muscles are affecting and improving while also teaching you the right way to do a specific exercise.

Use our app to do your workout without a personal trainer and free of cost. We are providing this application free of cost.
