Project Info

Shorten your long and irritating URLs using our application to easily share them in your social media accounts or anywhere. Shorted form of your long URLs looks elegant, clean and nice because user of your URL prefers shorter things.

Using this app you can easily track record of your shorted URLs and customize them as you like with our features mention below and exiting and easy to use UI.


ETUs - URL Shortner Features

  • Easily create short URL without account


  • Protect your URL with password and make more secure


  • Enable/Disable your URLs as per your needs


  • Keep record of your URL by checking features like Visitors count and more


  • Generate QR codes for your URLs


  • Edit or customize your URLs when you need


  • One-Click easy coping


  • Easy to use UI


  • Manage your all URLs in One Place


  • Dark Mode for your eye protection


  • Create new URLs in no time


  • Share your shorted URLs with one tap
